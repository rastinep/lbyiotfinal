What is GIS?

The Garbage Identification System is a garbage bin disposal tracking system within the campus of De La Salle University that aims to obtain information on the three criterias specified: garbage bins are full or not, checks if the lids are left open in garbage bins and monitors the type of garbage that enters (dry or wet).

What can it do?

This alert system aims to have a better scheduling and maintain the cleanliness within the campus garbage bins for the Building and Grounds Maintenance Office (BGMO). This system can be mobilized within the campus by placing sensor nodes in all of the garbage bins.

A garbage bin map is generated within the campus to see and monitor which garbage
bins are full. The staffs within the BGMO can access this data through a web site that shows
the generated map of the garbage that has the three criterias.
